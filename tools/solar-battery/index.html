Is a battery worth it — now, or after 2027?

Saldering (1:1 net metering) ends on January 1, 2027. That date matters more than almost any other number here — a battery's value changes completely once it hits. See both sides below.

No supplier has fixed this yet — it'll likely track wholesale prices, which move. Treat this as an illustrative range, not a quote.

Simplified model: assumes ~950 kWh/kWp/year (typical Dutch yield), a 30% self-consumption baseline without a battery, and a battery that can complete roughly 280 useful cycles a year. Real production depends on your roof's orientation, shading, and the weather that year — see our other guides for more precise options later.
